Our Adoption Conditions

Our mission is to ensure our Maine Coon kittens find the most loving and responsible homes possible. We carefully screen all future families to guarantee a safe and nurturing environment.

Minimum Adoption Requirements

  • Must be 19 years or older.
  • Stable home environment with proper ventilation and natural light.
  • Financial capability to support veterinary care, food, and grooming needs.
  • No plans to declaw the kitten ‚Äî this is strictly forbidden.
  • Commitment to keeping the kitten indoors or providing a secured outdoor space.

Kitten Availability Timeline

9 Weeks

First vet check, vaccination, microchipping, and socialization begins.

12-13 Weeks

Second vaccination, continued socialization, final vet approval.

14 Weeks & Up

Kittens are ready to join new families. Contract and ID verification required at this stage.

Legal Adoption Contract

A legally binding adoption contract is signed before pick-up or delivery. This contract ensures:

  • Non-breeding agreement (unless stated otherwise in writing)
  • Commitment to annual veterinary check-ups
  • Agreement to return the kitten to us if rehoming is necessary

Health Guarantee

Each kitten is sold with a 1-year genetic health guarantee. They are tested for common feline diseases (FeLV, FIV, PKD) and leave our home with:

  • Complete vet records
  • Vaccination certificates
  • Microchip documentation (registered in the U.S.)
  • Spay/Neuter certificate or agreement form

Final Notes

We are based in the United States and operate with full compliance under local animal welfare laws. Our cattery is TICA-registered and follows ethical breeding guidelines. Kittens are raised in a home environment with daily human interaction, playtime, and lots of love.

A non-refundable deposit of $261 is required to reserve a kitten. This confirms your commitment and enables us to proceed with documentation updates and final vet arrangements.
