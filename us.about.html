Our Story

At CO-MAINECOON Cattery, we are passionate about raising healthy, well-socialized Maine Coon kittens. Since our founding and passion, our mission has been to connect these majestic cats with loving families. Our cattery is a safe, clean, and joyful space where every kitten is nurtured like family.

We work for you and your pets. We do not keep our animals in cages, first of all they are members of our family, and only then the producers of the nursery, therefore we are very sensitive to their health, physical and psychological condition. Using the experience gained, we try to build our work in such a way that it always results in timely fed, happy pets and their satisfied owners.
